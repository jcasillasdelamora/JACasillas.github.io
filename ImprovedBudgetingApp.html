Personal Budgeting

Getting Started

To get started, you will simply input your yearly income, this can be done by calculation your income based on a weekly, biweekly salary or if you know how much you make in a year you can simply input that. Ones we establish your income then we can start by adding up all your living expenses, the expenses that you dont have the luxury to default. The end goal of this program is to make light of the money that has to be reserved in order to maintain those expenses and see how the remainder can be used to pay off debt or invest in your future, as well as to set a responsible going out and vacation fund
